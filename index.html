About Us

Spicy Den is an Authentic Spice and Indian Take Away with 28+ years of heritage. We service all your spice needs and are famous for our legendary Samoosas and a wide range of incense sticks. Authentic Durban curries are now available too! All our food is Halaal certified by the Halaal Foundation of South Africa."
